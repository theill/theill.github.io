The legislation in Denmark doesn\u2019t really fit well with how Uber operates so it\u2019s interesting to see what happens. The danish \u201ctaxiraad\u201d already pressed charges against them but let\u2019s see if anything is going to be done about it.<\/p>\n

Our customers, taxi companies, of course does not like this and a lot of drivers are publicly complaining about it and suggesting all kind of ways to stop it.<\/p>\n

It doesn\u2019t change the way Drivr operates – we will continue to work within the confines of the law and provide existing taxi companies with tools allowing them to improve their quality of service.<\/p>\n
